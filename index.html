Clipboard Cleaner 🔒

Why this tool matters:
Your clipboard stores data you copy (like passwords, messages, and personal info).
This tool clears that clipboard manually and automatically, helping protect your privacy.

🛠️ How to Use

  • Click the "🧹 Clear Clipboard" button anytime to immediately clear your clipboard.
  • This tool also automatically clears your clipboard every 30 seconds in the background.
  • You’ll see a message confirming your clipboard was successfully cleared.
